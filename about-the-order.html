About the Order

The Rose Croix is part of the Ancient and Accepted Rite, also known as the Scottish Rite, which is a branch of Freemasonry. The order consists of multiple degrees, with the Rose Croix being the 18th degree.

The Ancient and Accepted Rite is a Christian Order that requires its members to profess the Trinitarian Christian Faith. This means belief in the Holy Trinity - Father, Son, and Holy Spirit - which is fundamental to mainstream Christianity.

Membership Requirements

To join the Rose Croix, you must:

  • Be a Master Mason for at least one year
  • Profess the Trinitarian Christian Faith
  • Seek fellowship, companionship, and spiritual development
  • Have a sincere interest in the beautiful and thought-provoking ceremonies of the Order

The Rose Croix Experience

Members of the Rose Croix appreciate the Order for its:

  • Beautiful and meaningful ceremonies
  • Strong sense of Christian fellowship
  • Opportunities for spiritual reflection and growth
  • Welcoming and supportive community

New members are always welcome. If you would like to learn more about joining the Rose Croix or have any questions about the Order, please don't hesitate to contact us.
